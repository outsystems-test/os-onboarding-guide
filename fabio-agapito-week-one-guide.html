Common First-Week Questions

What is Agentic Systems Engineering and how does it shape the Digital team's work?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category announced March 31, 2026 — repositioning from low-code to the platform that governs, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI agents. For Digital, your internal tools and Big Bet experiments prove the concept from the inside. Read the ASE FAQ and watch the product sizzle demo.
What is the Big Bet I'm assigned to and where do I start?
Ask Gonçalo Fonseca for a Big Bet brief walkthrough on Day 1. Before the meeting, find the brief in Google Drive and read it top to bottom. Check the WIP experiment doc to understand what's already been tried.
When should I escalate a blocker vs. solve it myself?
Escalate to Gonçalo Fonseca when blocked for more than a day on something outside your control. For platform/API questions post in #ase-internal or #agent-workbench-dev. For infrastructure/access issues post in #digital-spot. Scout timelines are short — surface blockers early.
What's the most important thing to get right in the first 30 days?
Understand the problem before building the solution. Read every existing document about your Big Bet, interview at least 3 revenue team end users, and run experiments with clear pass/fail criteria before writing production code.
