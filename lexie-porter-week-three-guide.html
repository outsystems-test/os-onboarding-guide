Your Week — Day by Day

Mon
Account Day
Morning
Select your named accounts Priority
Finalise your named account list with Madeline Dibble. Use Deal Mate + Compass + Sales Navigator to validate ICP fit and identify the right entry contacts for each account.
Afternoon
Research contacts in named accounts
Build an initial contact map for your top 3 accounts — Economic Buyer, Champion candidate, and technical stakeholder. Use ZoomInfo and LinkedIn Sales Navigator.
EOD
Review peer calls in Gong
Listen to 1–2 calls from your mentor or top regional AEs focused on account entry. Listen for openers, discovery questions, and how they handle early objections.
Tue
Sales Play Day
Morning
Sales Play Tuesday Session Weekly
Attend and bring a specific question about an account in your territory. Apply the play's talk track to one real prospecting email you'll send this week.
Afternoon
Begin outreach to top accounts
Launch your first personalised outreach sequences in Outreach with your SDR. Aim for at least 3 accounts in active sequence by end of day. Use the Sales Play messaging and the Account Context Agent in Deal Mate for personalisation.
EOD
Begin responding to MQLs (if assigned)
Check if any inbound MQLs have been assigned to your territory. Prioritise quickly — speed to lead matters. Use the discovery flow from the Sales Playbook to qualify.
Wed
Shadow Day
All day
Shadow a live call High value
Madeline Dibble or your mentor should have arranged a live discovery or demo call for you to observe. Take structured notes: how they opened, pains uncovered, objections handled, next steps committed. Debrief after.
Afternoon
Review Competitive + Platform pages
Review the Competitive Page →, Industries and Solutions page, and Platform Product Marketing Hub on Seismic. Focus on content relevant to your named accounts' industries.
Thu
Agentic Thursday
Live session
Agentic Confidence Thursday Attend
Weekly enablement session. This week apply the session's content to at least one prospecting message for a named account.
Afternoon
Mentor 1:1 — Call Prep & Prospecting Best Practices
Discuss call prep techniques, how your mentor uses Outreach/EveryoneSocial/Sales Navigator, and their Pipeline Development Template. Get hands-on with how they build an outreach sequence for a cold account.
EOD
Explore Customer Stories in the Application Portfolio
Find 3 stories relevant to your named accounts' industries. These are your proof points in discovery conversations. Seismic Customer Stories →
Fri
Build Day
Morning
Build your weekly Pipeline Generation Plan Ongoing
Use the Pipeline Development Template to plan next week's prospecting activities. Include outreach volume, follow-up cadence, and any events or campaigns you can leverage. Share with your SDR.
Midday
End-of-week check-in with Madeline Dibble
Confirm introductions with extended team have occurred (SA, CS, RevOps). Share call shadow debrief. Review pipeline generation plan. Surface any blockers to getting first meetings.
EOD
Start your Champion Deck for Deal Clinic
Review the Champion Deck best practices and begin building one for your most qualified account or an account with the highest potential. Deal Clinic is in Week 4 — come with a real opp or a hypothesis.
