Common First-Week Questions

What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why does it matter for my deals?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category announced March 31, 2026. Instead of positioning as "low-code app dev," we're now the platform that governs, secures, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI. Every prospect you talk to is building AI agents — but most don't have a way to do it safely at scale. That's our opening. Read the ASE FAQ and watch the product sizzle video before your first customer call.
What Sales Plays should I be running in Q2?
Two vertical plays are live: Banking & Financial Services (BFS) and Insurance. A new play drops every Tuesday through May. Tag all relevant opportunities in Salesforce under Primary Sales Play. Check Seismic for the full playbook. Use Deal Mate — it now suggests the right play directly in your workflow.
What is Revenue Launchpad and will I go through it?
Revenue Launchpad is OutSystems' field-specific onboarding bootcamp, held in Lisbon. The most recent cohort completed April 29 – May 2, 2026. Graduates leave with a territory POV and account entry package. Ask Bobbie Jo Frazier when the next cohort is scheduled and whether you're enrolled. If you missed this cohort, the replays and materials are in the Revenue Hub Canvas in Slack.
How does the Q2 SPIFF work for me as an AE?
The Q2 Create & Close SPIFF pays 1.5× on quota AND commission for any new ARR opportunity created April 29, 2026 – May 2, 2026 that closes in Q2. As a Senior Business Development Representative, you're eligible for any deal source. Track performance on the Q2 SPIFF Dashboard. Questions → email sales.operations.team@outsystems.com.
What's the best way to handle "we'll just use AI to write code" from prospects?
This is now one of our most common objections. The answer: AI tools create a "hidden stack" of architecture and security debt. Challenge their low-upfront-cost assumption — ask how many specialist engineers it takes to get a prototype to secure production. Use the Competitive Playbook to go deeper. Proof points: Axos Bank (60% faster, 85% less rework), Petrobras (48 solutions in 8 months, 15× ROI).
Where do I get help with a technical question in a deal?
Three paths: (1) Your assigned SA, your Global SA — for anything specific to your deal. (2) #solutions_ama — SA team answers field questions here. (3) #ase-internal — for Agentic Systems Engineering questions. For customer references, ask in #customer-references.
What is the ONE Conference and how do I attend?
ONE '26 is the company's flagship customer and prospect conference, June 2–3, 2026. It's a major deal acceleration event — bring your best accounts. To attend, you must apply via the ONE '26 Internal App by April 24, 2026. Review the Revenue Team Attendance Guidelines first. There's also a €15,000 prize pool "ONE World Cup" for teams who bring the most registrations.
Where do I find the most current slide decks and sales content?
Seismic is the source of truth. Never use a slide deck you found in a random Google Drive folder — it may be outdated. The Revenue Hub Canvas pinned in #one-revenue-team indexes all active resources with direct links. For AI-powered Q&A on sales content, use NotebookLM — links are shared in the weekly Revenue Pulse posts.
