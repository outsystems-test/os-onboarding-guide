§ Month Two — Days 31–60
     

        Onboarding is behind you. Month 2 is about execution. You have accounts. You have sequences running. Now the job is to convert activity into pipeline — creating your first real opportunities, hosting your first New Business Meetings, and running your first complete discovery calls solo.      

     

        The target by end of month: 5 active opportunities (land & expand), 4 NBMs per week, and your 2nd Deal Clinic complete. Your manager will be in the room with you for your first 5 meetings — lean into that coaching window.      

     
        Month 2 theme         Explain OutSystems · Execute your pipeline plan · Master the tools that save you time selling
