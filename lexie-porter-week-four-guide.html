End of Month 1 — Common Questions

What if I don't have a live opportunity for the Deal Clinic?
That's fine. Build a hypothesis Champion Deck using one of your top named accounts. Pick the account where you've done the most research, identify who you believe the Champion and Economic Buyer would be, and map a plausible set of MEDDPICC values. Your mentor or manager can role-play the discovery call. The purpose is to practise the structure, not to submit a perfect deal.
How is the Skill Will framework used to evaluate me?
Skill Will is a coaching matrix that maps your ability (Skill) against your motivation and commitment (Will) across key competencies: messaging, prospecting, discovery, deal management, and execution. It's used by your manager in regular 1:1s and formal reviews. In month 2, expect Madeline Dibble to use this as the foundation for coaching. Come to your Month 1 review with a self-assessment of where you see your own gaps.
What does Month 2 look like?
Month 2 is your first full pipeline generation cycle. You should be running full prospecting cadences, attending live customer calls (not just shadowing), and progressing at least 2–3 opportunities to 10% stage. Revenue Launchpad (if not yet completed) should happen in this window. Your weekly 1:1 cadence with Madeline Dibble shifts from onboarding check-ins to deal reviews and pipeline inspection. Align on your 3 Month 2 priorities on Friday.
