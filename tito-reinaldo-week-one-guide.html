Common First-Week Questions

What is the Partner Center and what does my team actually own?
The Partner Center is OutSystems' internal platform supporting the partner ecosystem — covering the partner program and the Marketing Funds Reimbursement projects. Rodrigo Leonardo led this for 14 months before you. He's your knowledge transfer source through end of April. By May 1, you own the technical direction, architecture, and delivery of everything in this scope.
What is the GenAI Suite and how does my team use it?
GenAI Suite is OutSystems' internal AI backbone built by the AI Foundations team (Fábio Agapito, João Araújo). It provides LLM access, content layers, vector search, and agent infrastructure. Your team integrates via tool credentials (ClientID, Secret, xapikey). Rodrigo already has active requests in #digital-genai-team-dev-support for Partner Center tools — review them and plan your session with Fábio and João this week.
How do I track AI-assisted code for my V2MOM metrics?
When any team member uses Claude, Mentor, or Gemini to assist in writing code for a Jira story or task, they flag it using a dedicated field in Jira. The metric counts the percentage of work items (stories + tasks, excluding bugs) with this flag set. Your Q2 target is 10%, rising to 50% by year-end. Start flagging from Day 1 to build the habit — it's a new metric so baseline data starts now.
What is My OS and how is it different from regular ChatGPT/Claude?
My OS is OutSystems' internal AI assistant, upgraded in March 2026 with agent execution via a centralised Skills Catalog. It runs Claude Haiku 4.5 and GPT 5.1, and it's connected to internal knowledge sources. Unlike external tools, it can execute specialized internal agents (like the V2MOM Agent). It's your first port of call for internal policy questions, V2MOM guidance, and any task where you'd normally search the intranet.
What should I know about the Salesforce migration in May?
On May 16, 2026 (21:00 UTC) through May 17 (00:00 UTC), the Salesforce instance migrates to a new environment and will be read-only for that 3-hour window. Any Partner Center integrations that write to SFDC will fail during this time. Review all integration points with Rodrigo this week and plan for the maintenance window. Vanessa Ribeiro posted the full spec in #du-heads-tech-leads on April 20.
What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why does it matter for Digital?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category launched March 31, 2026 — repositioning OutSystems as the platform for governed, observable, scalable enterprise AI. The Digital team is Customer Zero: you build internal tools on OutSystems using Agent Workbench, GenAI Suite, and Mentor. Your work proves (or improves) the product the company sells. When you integrate AI into Partner Center, you're not just building an internal tool — you're a reference customer.
Where do I go when I'm stuck on a technical OutSystems question?
Three paths: (1) #solutions_ama — SA team answers field and tech questions here. (2) #digital-genai-team-dev-support — for AI integration issues. (3) AI Foundations Open Hours (ask Fábio to add you this week). For platform documentation, the OutSystems Community is the source of truth. For internal app architecture, Rodrigo is your human reference through end of April.
Do I need to publish a V2MOM as a Tech Lead?
Yes. All managers and tech leads are expected to publish a V2MOM. The March 31 deadline for managers has passed, but you've just joined — ask Carolina about your timeline and what goals to inherit vs. create.
