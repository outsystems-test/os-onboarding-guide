Common First-Week Questions

What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why does it matter for my work?
ASE is OutSystems' new category launched March 31, 2026 — we're the platform for governed, scalable enterprise AI. As a Digital engineer you build internal tools on this same platform. Understanding the ASE vision means building with product direction, not against it. Read the ASE FAQ and watch the sizzle video.
I have a system access issue — who do I contact?
Post in #digital-spot or message @Digital Spot. They handle Slack, GitHub, Jira, ODC tenant access, and app permissions. For urgent Day 1 issues, also flag Catarina directly.
What does the Digital team actually build?
Internal-facing tools and experiences on the OutSystems platform — products like Compass, Deal Mate, and automation tools that support the revenue and operations org. Ask Catarina for the current project portfolio in week 1.
What is ODC and how is it different from O11?
OutSystems Developer Cloud (ODC) is the cloud-native next-gen platform. O11 is the legacy version. The Digital team builds primarily on ODC. Key Q2 release: Logic Interoperability now lets O11 business logic run in ODC without AO count friction — useful if you're bridging any legacy systems.
How do I stay informed about what's happening at the company?
#one-revenue-team for the commercial pulse (Revenue Pulse every Monday and Thursday from Sarah Cropp) and #ase-internal for the product and engineering narrative. Catarina will share team updates in #digital.
What is the ONE Conference and should I attend?
ONE '26 is the flagship company conference, June 2–3, 2026. Apply via the ONE Internal App by April 24 — ask Catarina if it's relevant for your role.
Where do I find internal documentation and team runbooks?
Confluence is the source of truth for team docs, architecture decisions, and runbooks. Ask Catarina for the right space in week 1. For company-wide resources, Compass is the intranet.
How does the team work day-to-day — Agile, sprints, standups?
Ask Catarina in your Day 1 1:1. Expect sprint or kanban structure in Jira, async updates in Slack, and Zoom for syncs. Get added to recurring standups and planning ceremonies in week 1.
