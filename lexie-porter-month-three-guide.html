§ Month Three — Days 61–90

This is graduation month. You are no longer ramping — you are running a territory. Month 3 is about defensible deals, qualified opportunities, and building the habits that will define your performance for the rest of the year.

The expectations are real: 5+ meetings per week, 1 new opportunity per week, a Champion Deck for every new Land deal, and a yellow/green scorecard by month end. If you finish Month 3 with champions on every active opp and a QBR under your belt, you've completed your ramp.
