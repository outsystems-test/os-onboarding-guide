Common First-Week Questions

What does "Field Enablement — Americas" mean at OutSystems right now?
You're the first dedicated Americas field enablement manager. Bobbie Jo described your job as building programs that "move the needle" — which means Sales Play delivery, Revenue Launchpad facilitation, coaching cadences, and regional content localization for Americas reps. The infrastructure exists (Launchpad, Sales Play Tuesday, Agentic Thursdays); your job is to make sure the Americas field is getting the most out of it and to identify gaps that need new programs.
What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why do I need to understand it deeply?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category announced March 31, 2026. The company now positions as the platform that governs, secures, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI — not just low-code app dev. Every enablement session you design or facilitate should help reps be fluent in this new narrative. Read the ASE FAQ and watch the product sizzle video in week 1. You can't enable on it until you know it.
What is Revenue Launchpad and what's my role in it?
Revenue Launchpad is OutSystems' field-specific onboarding bootcamp, held in Lisbon. The April 2026 cohort (April 14–16) was the first-ever run — graduates left Day One ready with a POV and account entry package. As Sr. Manager, Field Enablement Americas, you own the Americas component of this program and are expected to evolve and potentially extend it. Get a full debrief from Bobbie Jo, Carolyn Birsky, and Paul Golding on what worked and what needs improving for the next cohort.
How does Sales Play Tuesday work and what's my role?
Every Tuesday through May, a new vertical or competitive sales play is launched to the entire field. Plays launched so far: BFS (April 8), Insurance (April 14), Compete vs. Pro-Code/AI-Native (April 21). Sessions run for each GEO — EMEA, AMER, APAC. You'll be co-delivering the AMER sessions or owning Americas-specific follow-on sessions. Get the upcoming play schedule from Bobbie Jo and understand what prep and content you need to contribute for each one.
What is the Agentic Confidence Thursdays series?
A weekly Thursday enablement series running through Q2 focused on building field fluency for AI-related conversations. Topics include Agent Workbench, Agentic Systems Engineering, competitive positioning, and value selling. You'll be delivering or co-delivering these for the AMER/EMEA sessions (11 AM ET). The APAC sessions run separately. Join this week's session to understand the format before you start owning delivery.
