Common First-Week Questions

What does "Customer Zero" actually mean in practice for the Digital team?
Customer Zero means Digital builds its own critical systems on OutSystems before — or instead of — buying third-party SaaS. It's both a strategic mandate and a sales asset. When you build something on OutSystems internally, you validate the product under real enterprise conditions, generate feedback for the Product team, and create a reference story that Sales can use. Your V2MOM has an explicit method (M5) to champion this and partner with Marketing to amplify the wins as case studies. Coordinate with Soraia Cassamo and the GTM team on the "OutSystems on OutSystems" programme.
What is the RevOps–Digital Big Bets collaboration and what do I need to do this week?
Madeline Dibble (SVP RevOps) launched a structured programme on April 7 to co-build 4 agentic solutions between Digital and RevOps using a "Scout" model — 2-week rapid prototyping with a Digital engineer and a business owner. The 4 tracks are: Actionable Prioritisation, Enablement/Onboarding, Data Management, and Productivity. You were named as the Digital executive sponsor and asked to advise on POCs for tracks 2–4. Review the #revops-digital-agent-big-bets-collab channel, read all messages since April 7, and confirm the Digital POCs for each track with your relevant direct reports.
What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why is it relevant to my CIO role?
ASE is OutSystems' new category launched March 31, 2026 — repositioning the platform as the system for governing, orchestrating, and scaling enterprise AI agents. As CIO you play a dual role: (1) Operator — you build agentic solutions internally to drive the company's AI transformation. (2) Evangelist — you speak to external CIO audiences about how you're building a governed agentic enterprise on OutSystems. Your V2MOM method M6 explicitly calls for moderating CIO roundtables and speaking at events like Gartner. Master the ASE narrative using the customer-facing slides and product sizzle demo.
How does the "Two-in-a-Box" model work and how do I enforce it?
Your V2MOM's first sub-method operationalises a co-leadership model: every major Digital initiative must have a Business Product Manager (owning the strategy and "Why") paired with a Digital Product Owner (owning the architecture and "How"). This is a cultural shift from request-taker to co-creator. The RevOps–Digital Big Bets collaboration is a live example of this model. For new initiative requests coming to your team, use the brief template Madeline Dibble developed — require business owners to fill it before Digital engineering resources are assigned.
What are the biggest strategic risks I need to know about from Day 1?
Your V2MOM identifies 7 obstacles. The three most urgent: (1) "Self-Funding Squeeze" — rising demand vs. fixed headcount creates velocity risk. Without an agile investment mechanism, high-ROI AI opportunities may stall. (2) "AI Readiness Gap" — autonomous agents require high data quality. Scale from pilots to production may exceed current capabilities in data integration and workforce skills. (3) "User Resistance & Leadership Inertia" — employees prefer familiar SaaS tools; leaders default to buying rather than building. Expect to navigate this actively in your first 90 days.
What is the Salesforce migration and what does my team need to do?
On May 16 (21:00 UTC) through May 17 (00:00 UTC), the OutSystems Salesforce instance migrates to a new environment and goes read-only for that 3-hour window. All write operations and API integrations that touch SFDC will fail during this window. Your IT ops lead Paulo Caetano should have a migration plan and comms strategy. Check with him this week: have all integration owners been notified? Do any batch jobs need to be paused? Is there a rollback plan?
How do I approach my first Digital All-Hands as CIO?
Lillian Romano runs the logistics — ask her to schedule your first All-Hands within your first 4–6 weeks. The format has been: in-person at Rocket Room (3rd floor, Lisbon), 2pm start, preceded by a finger food lunch at 1:30pm, with a Zoom option for remote attendees. Your first All-Hands should introduce your priorities, acknowledge the team's current momentum (ASE launch, Wi-Fi 7 rollout, Deal Mate delivery), and signal what "Customer Zero" means to you as the new CIO. Tone matters — the team responded warmly to leaders who engage directly and celebrate execution.
Where do I find out what my team is building and releasing each week?
Paulo Caetano posts a weekly Digital Technology Release Plan every week in #team-digital-pvt. It highlights key deployments, bug fixes, infrastructure milestones, and the teams responsible. Reading this every week keeps you current on delivery velocity without needing a status meeting. Recent highlights: Wi-Fi 7 rollout (5/10 offices), 95 Pricing Calculator defects resolved, Google Meet language detection deployment. This is your pulse check on whether your team is executing.
