Week 2 Questions

How do I qualify a deal using MEDDPICC?
MEDDPICC stands for Metrics, Economic Buyer, Decision Criteria, Decision Process, Paper Process, Identify Pain, Champion, Competition. At OutSystems, moving an opportunity to 10% requires confirmed Identified Pain and an initial Champion. At 25% you need Metrics and Decision Criteria defined. Review the MEDDPICC Cheat Sheet on Seismic and ask Josh Dixson for a worked example from a recent deal in your territory.
What should I know before my first mentor session?
Come prepared with your 30-second answer to "what does OutSystems do?" Your mentor's job in Week 2 is to help you refine that answer and discuss upcoming calls to shadow. Also bring 2–3 questions about territory accounts you've identified — your mentor may have history with some of them in Gong.
How do I prioritise my territory accounts?
Start with Compass Customer Scores for any existing OutSystems customers in your territory — high score means expansion potential, low score means risk. For new logos, use Deal Mate to run account research and Sales Play matching. Layer in LinkedIn Sales Navigator to identify trigger events (new CIO, digital transformation announcements, hiring spikes). Bring your tiering to Josh Dixson this week for validation.
