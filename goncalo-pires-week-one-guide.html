    <h2 class="section-title">Common First-Week Questions</h2>

    <div class="section-rule"></div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">What is Agentic Systems Engineering and how does it affect what I build?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">ASE is the platform category OutSystems announced on March 31, 2026 — repositioning from low-code to the platform that governs, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI agents. As an engineer, this means you're building the infrastructure that powers agent creation, execution, and governance at enterprise scale. Read the <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1u8t5rJ1N7iFvYEpmM6rkR0BYzK9vzMRHQg_7RO3-hxA/edit">ASE FAQ</a> and watch the <a href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QwSJnd-NSQU">product sizzle demo</a> to understand what you're building toward.</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">What's the engineering team's current sprint focus?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">Ask Sérgio Oliveira for the current sprint board link and the Q2 OKRs on Day 1 — these tell you what the team is optimising for right now. The broader roadmap lives in <a href="https://outsystems.enterprise.slack.com/docs/T041063TZ/F0ASM9HMVPC">the Product Roadmap doc</a>. If something isn't in the current sprint, it's either backlogged or deprioritised — don't assume it's being worked on.</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">What does the engineering onboarding program look like?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">Engineering onboarding at OutSystems is structured around milestones: first PR merged by end of week 1, first independent ticket shipped by end of week 2, first on-call rotation by end of month 1. Ask <a href="#">Sérgio Oliveira</a> for the full onboarding checklist and timeline. The programme is self-paced but your manager will check in on progress weekly.</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">How does the performance review and promotion cycle work?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">Reviews happen Twice a year. The <a href="[TBD — check People team portal]">Engineering Career Ladder</a> defines expectations at each level. Ask Sérgio Oliveira how your current level is defined and what 'exceeds expectations' looks like in your first 1:1 — don't wait until review season. Promotions are driven by demonstrated impact, not tenure.</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">How do I get a PR reviewed? What's the expected turnaround?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">This is now one of our most common objections. The answer: AI tools create a "hidden stack" of architecture and security debt. Challenge their low-upfront-cost assumption — ask how many specialist engineers it takes to get a prototype to secure production. Use the <a href="https://outsystems.seismic.com/apps/doccenter/1d265838-3b45-4228-a925-d8af6847942b/doc/%252Fdd031c7148-da5e-359f-6101-766b4eba5e90%252FdfODE4YTc4MDQtNDk5MS00MmU2LTllZmMtZWNlNDlmYmQ1NmY3%252CPT0%253D%252CSW50ZXJuYWw%253D%252Flffac43ed5-ba83-4823-8395-c212871fb392/grid/">Competitive Playbook</a> to go deeper. Proof points: Axos Bank (60% faster, 85% less rework), Petrobras (48 solutions in 8 months, 15× ROI).</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">Where do I get help when I'm stuck on a technical problem?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">Three paths: (1) <strong>##rd-release-engineering-support</strong> — ask your immediate team first. (2) <strong>#engineering-ama</strong> — the broader engineering org. (3) <strong>#ase-internal</strong> — for platform-specific questions about Agent Workbench or ASE. Before posting, search Confluence for existing ADRs or runbooks — the answer is often already documented.</div>

  </div>

  <div class="faq-item">

    <div class="faq-q">What is the ONE Conference and why does Engineering attend?</div>

    <div class="faq-a">ONE '26 is OutSystems' annual flagship customer conference, June 2–3, 2026. Engineers attend to demo new platform features, support customer technical sessions, and hear directly from the users of what you build. Apply via the <a href="https://outsystemsdt.outsystems.app/ONE26/Requestcreation">ONE '26 Internal App</a> by <strong>April 24 (applications closed)</strong>. Ask Sérgio Oliveira whether your team has dedicated slots and how attendance is decided.</div>
