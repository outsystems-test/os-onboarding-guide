Common First-Week Questions

What is Agentic Systems Engineering and what does it mean for People?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category announced March 31, 2026 — repositioning from low-code to the platform that governs, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI agents. For People, this means the talent profile the company needs is evolving: new engineering specialisms (AI/agents, LLM tooling, observability), new messaging for employer brand, and updated job architectures. Watch the product sizzle demo and read the ASE FAQ to understand the technical direction — it directly shapes the talent strategy you'll be executing.
What People programmes will I own, and where do I start?
Ask Sílvia Melo Gomes for a full handover on Day 1 — your assigned client groups, the open roles you own, the performance cycle status, and any active people situations. Before taking ownership of anything, read the People strategy doc and the headcount plan for your client groups. Your first job is to understand the current state: which roles are most critical, which people situations are most urgent, and which programmes are already in flight. Don't redesign anything in week 1 — understand what exists and why before making changes.
What does People onboarding look like and what are the milestones?
People onboarding at OutSystems is structured around your function. Typical milestones: HRIS and ATS access live by end of Day 1, People policies and handbook read by end of week 1, first client group 1:1s completed by end of week 2, and first independent hire, programme, or HRBP decision by month 1. Ask Sílvia Melo Gomes for the full onboarding timeline and what 'ready to operate independently' looks like for your specific function.
How is People performance measured?
People performance is measured on both operational metrics and business impact. Operational: time-to-fill, offer acceptance rate, engagement score, retention rate, and programme completion. Business impact: are the teams you support growing effectively, are managers improving, is the talent pipeline matching the company's strategic direction? Ask Sílvia Melo Gomes for your specific KPIs and targets this quarter. The People Career Ladder defines expectations at each level.
When should I escalate a People situation vs. handle it myself?
Escalate to Sílvia Melo Gomes immediately when: a situation involves potential legal risk (discrimination, harassment, wrongful termination), a senior leader is involved, an employee has expressed self-harm or serious distress, or a performance or disciplinary action could result in termination. Handle independently (with documentation): routine performance conversations, manager coaching, standard offer approvals within guidelines, and policy clarification questions. When in doubt, loop in your manager and Legal early — People situations that escalate unexpectedly are almost always the ones where someone waited too long to ask for help.
Where do I find People policies, frameworks, and programme documentation?
Three places: (1) Confluence — all People policies, the employee handbook, performance management framework, job architecture, and programme playbooks. Search here first before answering any manager or employee question. (2) Google Drive — People Folder — comp benchmarking data, org design working documents, engagement survey results, and L&D materials. (3) Workday — the authoritative source for employee records, compensation history, and org data. Never answer a comp or policy question from memory — always check the source.
What is the ONE Conference and what is People's role?
ONE '26 is OutSystems' annual flagship customer conference, June 2–3, 2026. For People, ONE is one of the year's most important employer branding moments — the company showcases its culture, talent, and ambition to customers, partners, and the market simultaneously. People supports ONE by coordinating employee attendance, managing the internal nomination and application process, and ensuring the employee experience at the event reflects the company's culture values. Ask Sílvia Melo Gomes what People specifically owns. Apply via the ONE '26 Internal App by April 24.
What's the most important thing to get right in the first 30 days?
Earn the trust of your client group leaders. In People, your effectiveness is entirely dependent on whether managers and business leaders believe you understand their context and can help them solve real problems. In your first 30 days: learn your client groups' business models, team structures, and talent gaps as well as they know them. Fill one open role, solve one manager problem, or run one programme that genuinely helps. The People professionals who have the most impact are the ones their client groups ask for by name — because they've shown they're on the team's side, not just enforcing process.
